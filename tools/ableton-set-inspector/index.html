ABOUT

          
A quick, safe overview of an Ableton Set.

        

        

          

            
What it does

            

              Shows the tracks, devices and audio files referenced by an Ableton Live Set, including plug-ins used, frozen tracks and
              whether audio is stored inside the project folder.
            

          

          

            
Why it’s useful

            

              Helps with collaboration and archiving by highlighting third-party plug-in dependencies, tracks that may need freezing and
              potentially missing audio files.
            

          

          

            
What it does not do

            
It cannot play the Set, check licences, detect installed plug-ins or confirm that external files still exist.
